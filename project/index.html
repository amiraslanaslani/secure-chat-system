How to Use Secure Chat:
  1. Click Enter Chat below to access the chat room.
  2. Choose a channel name and set a password to create or join a private channel.
  3. Share the channel name and password with your friends to chat together securely.
  4. All messages are encrypted before they leave your device. Only those with the correct password can read them.
About Client-Side Encryption:
  • Messages are encrypted in your browser using your channel password before being sent to the server.
  • Decryption happens only on your device. No one else (not even server admins) can read your messages without the password.
  • Even if the server is compromised, your conversations remain private and secure.
  • This ensures zero-knowledge privacy: only you and those you trust have access to your messages.
